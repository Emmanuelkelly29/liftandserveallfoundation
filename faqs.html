FAQ Categories

Donations

How can I donate from abroad?

We accept global donations securely via PayPal, Stripe, and Flutterwave. You can also make bank transfers to our foundation account. All donations are tax-deductible and we provide receipts for your records.

How do I know my donation is being used properly?

We provide regular transparency reports and updates showing how donations are allocated. You can view our annual financial reports, impact updates, and program evaluations on our website. We maintain complete transparency in all our operations.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! We offer monthly recurring donations that provide consistent support to keep children in school. You can set up recurring donations through our online donation form or contact us directly to arrange automatic payments.

What is the minimum donation amount?

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference. Even $25 can provide school supplies for one child for a month.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Lift and Serve All Foundation are tax-deductible. We provide official receipts for all donations that you can use for tax purposes.

Volunteering

Can I volunteer if I'm outside the country?

Yes! You can volunteer virtually or join us during major outreach programs. We offer online tutoring, content creation, translation services, and technical support opportunities that you can do from anywhere in the world.

What skills do I need to volunteer?

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Whether you have teaching experience, technical skills, administrative abilities, or simply a passion for education, there's a volunteer opportunity for you.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary depending on the volunteer role. Some opportunities require just a few hours per week, while others are project-based. We're flexible and can work with your schedule.

Do I need a background check to volunteer?

Background checks are required for volunteers who will have direct contact with children. For virtual or administrative roles, background checks may not be necessary.

Programs

How do you select students for scholarships?

We select students based on financial need, academic potential, and community recommendations. Our selection process includes family interviews, school assessments, and community verification to ensure we're helping those who need it most.

What communities do you serve?

We primarily serve rural and underserved communities in Ghana, focusing on areas where educational infrastructure is lacking. We've reached over 10 communities and continue to expand our reach.

How do you measure the impact of your programs?

We measure impact through student academic performance, attendance rates, graduation rates, and community feedback. We also conduct regular evaluations and publish impact reports showing our progress.

Can I sponsor a specific student?

Yes! We offer individual student sponsorship programs where you can support a specific child's education. You'll receive regular updates about their progress and can even correspond with them.

Partnerships

How can my organization partner with you?

We welcome partnerships with corporations, educational institutions, NGOs, and government agencies. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities that align with your organization's goals and our mission.

What are the benefits of partnering with you?

Partners receive tax-deductible benefits, recognition in our materials, regular impact reports, networking opportunities, and employee engagement programs. We also provide comprehensive reporting on how partnership funds are used.

Can schools partner with you?

Absolutely! We partner with schools for student exchanges, resource sharing, teacher training, and joint educational programs. Contact us to discuss how your school can get involved.

General

How can I stay updated on your work?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube), or visit our News & Blog section regularly. We also send monthly impact updates to our supporters.

How can I visit your programs?

We organize regular visits to our programs for donors, volunteers, and partners. Contact us to arrange a visit and see our work in action firsthand.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes! We accept books, school supplies, computers, tablets, educational games, and other learning materials. Contact us to discuss what items we currently need and how to arrange delivery.

How can I get involved if I'm not in Ghana?

You can donate online, volunteer virtually, help with fundraising in your community, or become an advocate for our cause. We have supporters and volunteers from around the world.

Is Lift and Serve All Foundation a registered organization?

Yes, Lift and Serve All Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. We operate with full legal compliance and maintain all necessary registrations and certifications.
